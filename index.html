Rules
  • The hunt will last for 48 hours from {start-time} to {end-time}.
  • The answers are not case sensitive and can contain special characters.
  • A minor typo will result in an incorrect answer, so be doubly sure of what you Enter. For example, if the answer is “codewarriors” and you enter “code wariors”, it will be considered incorrect.
  • Hints will be posted on our discord server.
  • Spamming the admins for hints is frowned upon.
  • The use of any unfair means will be dealt with strictly and you shall be permanently disqualified from participating.
  • There is no restriction on the number of times you can enter an answer, so feel free to guess, although random answers are highly unlikely to work.
  • If you feel you’re stuck at any point it’s best to clear your head and starting thinking from scratch.
  • Refer to the learning resources if you are new to cryptic hunts.
  • Have fun playing and don’t panic.
